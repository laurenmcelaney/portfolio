About


As a seasoned product designer, my journey began at the tender age of 13 when I embarked on a self-taught odyssey in coding with HTML and CSS, igniting my passion for web design. Crafting websites for sheer enjoyment became a foundational part of my creative expression from that early age. This fervor for design evolved into a commitment to curate delightful user experiences, driven by a deep-seated empathy cultivated through immersive experiences in customer service. My tenure in this realm fostered a profound understanding of user needs and behavior, enriching my approach to product design with a profound empathetic touch. With a fusion of technical prowess and a keen eye for user-centric solutions, I endeavor to create products that not only function seamlessly but also resonate on an emotional level, enriching the lives of those who interact with them.
